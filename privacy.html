Glowherd Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-20

What we collect

Glowherd does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The app does not require an account.

Advertising

Glowherd displays advertisements served by Google AdMob and may request the user's permission to use the device's advertising identifier via App Tracking Transparency. AdMob and its advertising partners may collect device identifiers, IP address, advertising interaction data, and similar non-personal information to deliver and measure ads. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy. Users in the EU/UK are presented with a consent form on first launch to choose whether personalized ads may be served.

In-app purchases

The optional "Remove Ads" purchase is handled by Apple's StoreKit. Glowherd does not see your Apple ID, payment information, or contact information.

Children

Glowherd is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
